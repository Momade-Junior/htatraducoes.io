Languages

Our translators are native speakers with the language skills needed to provide services in the Mozambican languages ​​highlighted. They are able to translate your documents, videos and meetings with ease and ability to handle all technical levels.

Emakhuwa 

The Emakhuwa language has about 4,097,788 speakers in the provinces of Cabo Delgado, Nampula, Niassa, Sofala and Zambézia.

Portuguese 

The Portuguese language has about 1,693,024 speakers in all the provinces of the country.

Xichangana 

The Xichangana language has about 1,660,319 speakers in the provinces of Gaza, Maputo, Maputo city and Niassa.

Cisena 

The Cisena language has about 1,218,337 speakers in the provinces of Manica, Sofala, Tete and Zambézia.

Cinyungue 

The Cinyungue language has about 457,292 speakers in the provinces of Manica and Tete.

Xirhonga

The Xirhonga language has about 239,307 speakers in the provinces of Gaza, Maputo, Maputo city and Inhambane.

OUR PROFESSIONALS

WHAT DO WE DIFFERENCE?

The HTA translation professionals are selected based on their skills and experiences. Why choose HTA?

Mozambique is a multilingual country and the HTA translations can help you communicate with everyone. Much more than simply breaking down language barriers, HTA translations can help you tailor your message to the wonderful cultural diversity of this great country. We make their ideas come naturally as if they were initially produced in the target language, making the translated text favorable to the target audience.
